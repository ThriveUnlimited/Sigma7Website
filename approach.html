01 — Approach

A few principles we don't compromise on.

Twenty years of building products has narrowed our convictions, not broadened them. These three principles set the standard every release answers to.

/ 01

Product over process

Every release answers to a single question — does the product justify itself? If it doesn't, we keep working until it does. No exceptions, no shortcuts.

/ 02

Patient iteration

Our cycles are measured in quarters, not sprints. A product ships when it stops asking to be revised — not when the calendar says it's time.

/ 03

Owned and answerable

Veteran-owned and privately held by the people who run it. No quarterly pressure, no committee design — the work and the consequences sit on the same shoulders.

A product company. Quietly built, since 2004.
