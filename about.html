03 — About

Twenty years in one place, doing one thing.

A storied company, told plainly. We started in 2004 and we're still here — same town geography, same standards, same distinct catalog.

Sigma7 Group was founded in 2004 with a simple premise — build a small portfolio of products, get each one right, and stay small enough to mean it.

Two decades later, we still operate from Massachusetts, still answer to the people who own the company, and still ship a deliberately distinct catalog. Some of the work is public; some isn't. All of it is held to the same standard.

We're a veteran-owned business — a detail we mention plainly because the values that come with it (discipline, accountability, mission focus) show up in the work. Whether we name them or not, the pride is there in all we deliver.
